About Me

I am a passionate linguist dedicated to unraveling the complexities of language through rigorous research and fieldwork. Currently pursuing my PhD at Leiden University, I am deeply engaged in the study of East Cushitic languages, with a particular focus on their phonological and morphological aspects.

Education

  • PhD in Linguistics, Leiden University, Netherlands (2020 - present)
  • MA in Linguistics, University of Cape Town, South Africa (2017 - 2019)
  • BA in Linguistics (Honors), University of Khartoum, Sudan (2007 - 2012)

Areas of Expertise

  • Phonological and morphological studies
  • East Cushitic languages
  • Linguistic fieldwork
  • Historical linguistics
  • Language documentation

Research

Current Projects

  • A Lexical and Phonological Reconstruction of East Cushitic
  • Early Linguistic History of East Africa (LHEAf) Project

Fieldwork Experience

  • Tanzania (2021 - 2022): Collected data from 8 languages
  • Sudan (2016 - 2018): Collected data on the Tegem language
  • Tagoi Language Orthography Development Project (2015 - 2018)
