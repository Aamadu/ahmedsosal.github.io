About Me

I am a linguist interested in exploring the complexities of language through rigorous research and fieldwork. Currently pursuing my PhD at Leiden University, I am deeply engaged in the study of East Cushitic languages, with a particular focus on their phonological and morphological aspects from a historical perspective.

Fields of interest

  • Phonological and morphological studies
  • Cushitic languages
  • Sudanese Arabic
  • Descriptive Linguistics
  • Historical linguistics
  • Language documentation

Cushitic Database

The (East) Cushitic Database is a comprehensive resource for researchers, linguists, and anyone interested in Cushitic languages. It contains detailed information on phonology, morphology, and lexicons of various Cushitic languages.

Features

  • Comprehensive word lists
  • Phonological inventories
  • Morphological featutes
  • Comparative Data
  • Historical linguistic data

Languages (Mainly EC)

  • Saho, Afar
  • Rendille, Somali, Maay, Boni
  • Arbore, Dhaasanac, Elmolo, Bayso
  • Hadiyya, Kambaata, Sidaama, Gedeo, Burji
  • Oromo, Diraytata, Mositacha, Konso
  • Gawwada, Ts'amakko, Yaaku
  • Dahalo
